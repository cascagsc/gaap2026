Additional Resources

Job Boards for Astronomy Backgrounds

  • GC Jobs is the Government of Canada job board that includes a variety of jobs across government agencies.

  • Work With Indies is a job board dedicated to small, independent video game development studios.

  • The AAS Job Registry is a job board run by the American Astronomical Society that includes non-academic jobs.

  • Women in Games is a non-profit dedicated to building a fair and equitable playing field for women and girls in games. They also run a Discord server where many job opportunities are shared (allies of all genders welcome!)

  • Tech Jobs For Good is a tech job board dedicated to postings for mission-driven companies and non-profits working on pressing social and environmental issues.

Turning Science

  • Turning Science aims to bridge the academia-industry gap to help PhDs market their skills and prepare for the hiring proces with interview practice, networking strategies, and more. They offer short 90-minute workshops along with more intensive day-long trainings.

The Erdős Institue

  • The Erdős Institue is a multi-university collaboration dedicated to helping PhDs secure fulfilling jobs at every stage of their career. The programming is offered in cohorts, 3 times per year, and runs entirely on-line. There are both recorded and live lectures for their certificate programs available!

  • There is one more cohort this year scheduled for Fall 2026 with an enrollment deadline of September 9th. The EXPLORE Level Programs are free to register!

Scientific Research and Experimental Development Tax Incentives

  • The SR&ED tax incentives are one of the government's largest programs for funding industrial research and development. Companies use these credits to fund research departments, often testing developments on large datasets like astronomical data. Targeting companies partially funded through these credits is a great strategy for finding research positions.

Research Computing Teams Newsletter

  • The Research Computing Teams Newsletter Archive is an archive of a weekly newsletter with nearly 200 entries all about research computing in industry.

Graduate and Postdoctoral Development Network Career Symposium

  • The Graduate and Postdoctoral Development Network Career Symposium is an annual virtual symposium aimed at professional development and career exploration. Previous years' programs and agendas are available on the site, with the 2026 symposium expected sometime in the Fall. Other resources are also available on their website.
